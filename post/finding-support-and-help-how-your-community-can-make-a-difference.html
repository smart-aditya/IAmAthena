    4 Forms of Target Harassment

    Updated: Jan 28


    1. Workplace Harassment


    This is one of the most common harassments of all and can ruin a great job and turn a company into a toxic and unproductive environment. Often, harassment goes unreported, as victims may be unsure of what qualifies as workplace harassment and what to do when they experience it. It can comprise of 6 components ; sexual harassment, intimidation, ridicule, assault, offensive pictures, offensive jokes.


    2. Street Harassment


    Street harassment includes unwanted comments, gestures, or acts directed at someone in a public space without their consent. It includes components like groping, unwanted touching, following, whistling, staring, flashing.


    3. Online Harassment


    This a millennium form of harassment which arises due to increased dependency on social media platforms. It can take forms of cyber bullying, cyberstalking , trolling.


    4. Verbal Harassment


    It can be a result of personality differences which end up in conflicts that have escalated from the casual eye roll to something serious. Unlike many of the discriminatory harassments, verbal abuse between people is not illegal. It usually manifests as someone who is often unpleasant and rude. Because of this reason, verbal harassment can often be damaging and demoralizing to people who refuse to co-operate with the abusive person.


    There maybe many forms of harassment however these are the broad categorization which sub include other forms as well. Lets have a look on forms of sexual harassment in public spaces(SHiPS).
